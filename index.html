Anubha Kothari

Anubha is a life and leadership coach who supports people in transforming themselves so they can have the most authentic, love-filled, and empowered human experience possible. A master of reinvention, she has navigated multiple transitions herself, from being a PhD student to product manager and now coach. An east-west blend and a student of life, she has forever been curious about what makes people the way they are and how they can live life fully.
